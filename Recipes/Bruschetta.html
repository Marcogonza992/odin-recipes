Bruschetta recipe

Grilled bruschetta chicken is a delicious marriage of bruschetta and grilled chicken breasts./p>

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tomatoes, cored
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat.
  2. chop tomatoes .
  3. Place croutons in a resealable plastic bag.
  4. Place chicken breasts on a plate and pat dry with paper towels.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Top each grilled chicken breast with bruschetta and sprinkle on crumbled croutons. Garnish with additional fresh basil.
